Who we are?

We are group of fans dedicated to retro gaming. We created this website to serve as a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in the golden era of video games. The website aims to provide visitors with insightful articles, detailed guides, and the latest news related to retro gaming and emulation. Features of the website include: Game Database: A curated list of classic games complete with descriptions, release information, and platform availability. Emulator Guides: Step-by-step tutorials on how to set up and use various emulators to play retro games on modern hardware. Community Forum: A space for members to discuss their favorite games, share tips, and support each other's retro gaming endeavors. Events Calendar: Information on upcoming retro gaming conventions, tournaments, and meet-ups. Reviews and Ratings: In-depth analyses of retro games and hardware, along with user-submitted ratings. Compatibility Lists: Resources to help users understand which games run on which emulators and any potential issues they might encounter. By building this website, the group hopes to preserve the nostalgia and joy that retro games bring, making it easier for newcomers and veterans alike to explore the vast universe of retro gaming and emulation.
